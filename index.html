Welcome to UW Pride Circle!

A safe and supportive community for LGBTQ+ students and allies at the University of Washington.

Privacy Mode Guide

Ghost Mode 👻 – Your profile is hidden from other users, and you would appear as 'anonymous' to other users. You can browse the platform privately without others knowing you're online.

Semi-Open Mode 😶‍🌫️ – Your profile is visible, but only to certain users (e.g., friends, mutual connections, or approved members). Others may see limited details unless you interact with them.

Mentor Mode 🎓 – Your profile is public to those seeking guidance. Users can easily find and message you for mentorship opportunities, and your status signals openness to offering advice or support.
