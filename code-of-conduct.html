UW PrideCircle's Code of Conduct

Acceptance is important to us. All members/users of the application must be respectful towards all others regardless of background, views or personal identities.

Inappropriate comments/jokes that target or mock others are unacceptable and go against our community values of inclusivity and mutual respect.

Users found in violation of these guidelines may have their accounts suspended without judgment or warning.

By signing below, you consent to follow these principles and understand that failure to do so may result in account termination.
