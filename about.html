About


UW Pride Circle is a LGBTQ+ student community and support platform for students at UW.

Our mission is to provide a confidential and welcoming space for connection, growth, and emotional well-being.


We prioritize user privacy and confidentiality. Logging in with a UW NetID is solely for verification purposes to ensure that all members are current UW students for security reasons. We do not track or share user activity, and profiles remain private within the community.

PrideCircle is safe, and we are committed to maintaining a secure and inclusive environment for all members.
